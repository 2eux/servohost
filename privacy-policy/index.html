ServoHost Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ServoHost (the **"Service,"** available at https://servo.host) handles your information. We are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your data. We only collect information strictly necessary for you to log in and use our Service effectively.

1. Data We Collect

Information from Google Sign-In (OAuth)

When you use your Google Account to sign in, we collect the following **minimum information** required for authentication and account creation:

  • **Name:** To identify your account and personalize your experience within the Service.
  • **Email Address:** To create your unique user account and contact you regarding service or security issues.
  • **Profile Picture (if available):1** For display on your profile within the Service.

This Google user data is used **ONLY** for authentication, user identification, and account management.

Usage Data

We automatically collect standard technical usage logs (metadata), which is essential for security and monitoring the health of the Service. This includes your **IP address**, **browser type**, and **timestamps** related to your activity.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information exclusively for the following purposes:

  • **Account Management:** Creating and managing your unique user account and profile.
  • **Service Operation:** Providing and maintaining the core functionality of the ServoHost service.
  • **Security and Integrity:** Protecting the security, stability, and integrity of the service against unauthorized access or abuse.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties.

We may share your data only with essential **third-party service providers** (such as cloud and web hosting companies) who require the information to perform technical services on our behalf (e.g., operating the servers). These providers are contractually required to keep your information confidential and use it only for providing the requested services.

4. Your Control

You have full control over your data. You can manage or delete your account and associated data by contacting us directly at the email address provided below.

If you request to delete your account, we will permanently delete your stored personal data within 30 days of the request.
